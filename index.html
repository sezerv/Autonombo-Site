About

Autonombo is an emerging technology initiative focused on autonomous mobility and intelligent robotic systems.

With many years of combined experience in academia and industry, we develop systems that operate reliably in complex, real-world environments.

Approach

Autonomy is a system-level challenge. From perception to decision-making and control, every layer must function seamlessly under real-world conditions.

Our focus is on building robust, scalable, and deployable systems — not just prototypes.

Status

Autonombo is currently in its formation phase. We are actively shaping our first systems and collaborations, with initial deployments planned in the near future.
